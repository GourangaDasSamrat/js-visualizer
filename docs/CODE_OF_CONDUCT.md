# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of **JS Visualizer** pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy and kindness toward other community members
- Giving credit where credit is due

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
